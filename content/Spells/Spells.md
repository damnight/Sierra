# Shield Ally
_2nd-level abjuration_
**Casting Time:** 1 reaction, which you take when your ally is hit by an attack or targeted by the _[magic missile](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:magic-missile)_ spell  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects one of your allies you can see within range. Until the start of your next turn, they have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and they take no damage from _[magic missile](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:magic-missile)_.

**_At Higher Levels._** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the number of allies you can affect increases by one per level.

**_Spell Lists._**  [Cleric](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:cleric), [Sorcerer](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:sorcerer), [Wizard](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:wizard)

# Shadow Clone
*4th-level illusion*
**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You summon 3 Shadow Clones in a range of 30 feet around yourself. Each Shadow Clone has 1 hp and an AC of 10 + your spellcasting modifier, and a movement of 0. When you cast this spell you can immediately change position with one of your clones and do so again as a bonus action on your subsequent turns. If you or your Shadow Clones are hit by an attack or about to take damage you can change position with one of your Shadow Clones as a reaction. 
When you lose concentration, all Shadow Clones disappear immediately.

**_At Higher Levels._** When you cast this spell using a 5th-level spell slot, your range in which you can summon your Shadow Clones increases to 60 feet and you summon 5 Shadow Clones.

**_Spell Lists._** [Bard](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:bard),  [Fighter - Eldritch Knight](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:eldritch-knight), [Rogue - Arcane Trickster](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:arcane-trickster), [Sorcerer](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:sorcerer),[Warlock](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:warlock), [Wizard](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spells:wizard)
